# Financial Policy

> Cash is oxygen. These policies keep the company solvent, honest, and audit-ready.

## Principles

- **Separate company and personal finances** absolutely. Dedicated business accounts only.
- **Every transaction is recorded** with a receipt/invoice and category.
- **Spend against budget**, not vibes. Authorize spend per the approval matrix.
- **Protect runway.** Decisions consider cash impact and runway.

## Spend approval matrix (illustrative)

| Amount | Approver |
|---|---|
| Small / routine (under threshold A) | Function lead |
| Medium (threshold A–B) | Founder |
| Large / strategic (over threshold B) | Co-founders (unanimous) |

Set thresholds A and B explicitly based on runway and revise as the company scales.

## Bookkeeping & accounting

- Use accounting software from day one; reconcile monthly.
- Engage an accountant/bookkeeper for compliance and tax.
- Maintain a clear chart of accounts.
- Monthly close: P&L, balance sheet, cash-flow review.

## Invoicing & receivables

- Invoice promptly per SOW terms.
- Track receivables; follow up on overdue invoices without delay.
- Require deposits/milestone payments on projects.

## Payroll & taxes

- Pay people and taxes on time, always.
- Set aside tax obligations; never spend money owed to authorities.

## Reporting

- Founders review a financial dashboard at least monthly: revenue, burn, runway, AR/AP, margin.
