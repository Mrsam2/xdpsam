# Responsible AI Usage Policy

> XDP technologies builds and uses AI. We do so responsibly, securely, and transparently.

## Using AI tools internally

- **Allowed and encouraged** for productivity (coding assistants, drafting, research), within these rules.
- **Never paste secrets, credentials, customer data, or proprietary code** into third-party AI tools that are not approved and contractually covered.
- Treat AI output as a **draft**: review, verify, and own it. You are accountable for what you ship.
- Verify facts and citations; AI can hallucinate.
- Respect licensing: do not introduce code with incompatible licenses.

## Approved tools

- Maintain a list of approved AI tools and the data classifications permitted for each.
- Prefer enterprise tiers with data-protection guarantees (no training on our data).

## Building AI features (for customers)

- **Transparency:** disclose when users interact with AI.
- **Human oversight:** keep a human in the loop for consequential decisions.
- **Fairness:** test for and mitigate bias in models and data.
- **Privacy & security:** apply data-protection rules to training and inference data.
- **Explainability:** be able to explain, at a reasonable level, how outputs are produced.
- **Safety:** guard against misuse, prompt injection, and harmful outputs.

## Governance

- Significant AI design choices recorded as ADRs.
- Periodically review AI tools and policies as the landscape and regulation evolve.
