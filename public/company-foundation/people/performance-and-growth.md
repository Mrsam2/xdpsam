# Performance & Growth

## Philosophy

Performance management is **continuous coaching**, not an annual surprise. The goal is growth, clarity, and high performance, not bureaucracy.

## Cadence

| Ritual | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1:1s | Weekly/bi-weekly | Coaching, blockers, feedback, growth |
| Goal check-in | Monthly | Progress vs. role outcomes / OKRs |
| Performance review | Quarterly (lightweight) | Calibrate, recognize, course-correct |
| Growth/career review | Twice a year | Development, promotion readiness |

## Expectations & feedback

- Set clear, written outcomes per role (tie to OKRs).
- Feedback is **timely, specific, and actionable** (Situation-Behavior-Impact).
- Praise in public, correct in private.

## Ratings & calibration

- Use a simple scale (e.g., Below / Meets / Exceeds expectations) per outcome.
- Calibrate across managers to ensure fairness and consistency.

## Growth & promotion

- Promotion = consistently operating at the next level for a sustained period.
- Each promotion is documented with evidence against the leveling framework.
- Invest in development: learning budget, stretch projects, mentorship.

## Performance improvement

- When someone is underperforming, address it early and directly.
- Use a clear, supportive improvement plan with defined goals and timeline.
- Be humane and lawful; document throughout.
