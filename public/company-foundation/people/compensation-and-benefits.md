# Compensation & Benefits

> Principle: **fair, transparent, and consistent.** Pay is set by role, level, and impact, not by negotiation skill.

## Compensation philosophy

- **Benchmark to market** for our stage and geography; aim for a defined target percentile.
- **Banded, leveled framework.** Each role maps to a level with a salary range.
- **Total compensation = salary + equity + benefits.** Early-stage may weight equity higher to preserve cash.
- **No pay secrecy traps.** Bands and the leveling framework are shared internally.

## Leveling framework (illustrative)

| Level | Scope | Autonomy |
|---|---|---|
| L1 | Executes well-defined tasks | Guided |
| L2 | Owns features/workstreams | Independent on scoped work |
| L3 | Owns outcomes across a domain | High; sets local direction |
| L4 | Drives strategy in a function | Sets direction; mentors |
| Lead | Owns a function + people | Accountable for results |

## Reviews & adjustments

- Compensation reviewed at least annually and at major scope changes/promotions.
- Promotions are based on demonstrated, sustained operation at the next level, not tenure.

## Benefits (build out as runway allows)

- Paid time off with a clear minimum and a "please actually take it" culture.
- Sick leave and parental leave per local law (meet or exceed).
- Learning & development budget per person/year.
- Home-office / equipment stipend for remote staff.
- Health coverage where legally/financially feasible.

## Pay practices

- Pay on time, every time. Cash discipline never delays payroll.
- All compensation changes documented and approved (Tier 1).
