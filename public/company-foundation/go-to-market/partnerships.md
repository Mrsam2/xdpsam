# Partnerships

## Why partnerships

Partnerships extend our reach, credibility, and capability without scaling headcount linearly. They can be a high-trust, low-CAC source of revenue.

## Partnership types

| Type | Description | Value |
|---|---|---|
| **Referral** | Partners send us leads (and vice versa) | Pipeline |
| **Technology / platform** | Certifications with cloud/SaaS vendors | Credibility + co-marketing |
| **Delivery / subcontracting** | Trusted firms we collaborate with on capacity | Scale delivery |
| **Reseller / channel** | Partners sell our products/services | Distribution |

## Evaluating a partner

- Strategic fit and shared values.
- Complementary (not competing) strengths.
- Reputation and reliability.
- Clear, mutual value exchange.

## Governance

- Every partnership has a written agreement (terms, IP, confidentiality, revenue share, exit).
- Define ownership of the relationship and review cadence.
- Founder approval for any binding partnership (Tier 1).
- Track partner-sourced revenue to know what's working.
