# Revenue Model

> How XDP technologies makes money. We start with cash-generating services and evolve toward higher-margin, recurring revenue.

## Revenue streams

| Stream | Description | Margin | Predictability |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Consulting / project services** | Custom software, cloud/DevOps, AI enablement, security | Medium | Low (project-based) |
| **Productized services** | Fixed-scope, fixed-price packages | Medium-high | Medium |
| **Retainers / managed services** | Ongoing support, MSP/DevOps-as-a-service | Medium-high | High (recurring) |
| **SaaS products** | Subscription software | High | High (recurring) |
| **Licensing / IP** | License internal tools/accelerators | High | Medium |
| **Training / EdTech** | Workshops, courses | Medium | Medium |

## Revenue evolution (the staircase)

1. **Phase 1 — Services for cash.** Win projects to fund the company and learn customer problems. Bootstrap-friendly.
2. **Phase 2 — Productize.** Convert repeatable work into fixed packages; raise margins and predictability.
3. **Phase 3 — Recurring.** Move clients to retainers/managed services for stable MRR.
4. **Phase 4 — Products.** Launch SaaS built on proven patterns for scalable, high-margin recurring revenue.

## Key metrics to track

- **Services:** utilization, effective hourly rate, project margin, pipeline coverage.
- **Recurring:** MRR/ARR, churn, net revenue retention, LTV, CAC, payback period.
- **Company:** gross margin, burn, runway, revenue concentration (avoid over-reliance on one client).

## Healthy-business guardrails

- No single client should exceed a defined % of revenue (concentration risk).
- Price for value, not just cost-plus.
- Protect cash: invoice promptly, manage receivables, require deposits on projects.
