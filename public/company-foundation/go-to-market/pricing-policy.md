# Pricing Policy

## Pricing philosophy

- **Price on value delivered**, not hours burned, wherever possible.
- **Be transparent and consistent.** Avoid one-off deals that erode standards.
- **Protect margin.** Discounts are deliberate, approved, and time-bound.

## Pricing models we use

| Model | When to use |
|---|---|
| **Time & materials** | Ambiguous scope, discovery, evolving needs |
| **Fixed price** | Well-defined scope and deliverables |
| **Value-based** | Clear, quantifiable customer value |
| **Retainer** | Ongoing support / managed services |
| **Subscription (SaaS)** | Productized recurring software |

## Setting prices

1. Know your cost floor (fully-loaded cost to deliver).
2. Understand the customer's value/ROI.
3. Position against alternatives.
4. Set a rate card with bands per role/seniority for services.
5. For SaaS, tier by value metric (seats, usage, features).

## Discount governance

- Standard rate is the default.
- Discounts beyond a set threshold require founder approval (Tier 1).
- Always trade discounts for something (longer term, case study, upfront payment).

## Payment terms

- Require deposits/upfront for projects.
- Net terms defined in the SOW/MSA; chase overdue invoices promptly.
- SaaS billed in advance (monthly/annual), annual preferred for cash flow.
