# Marketing Strategy

## Positioning

XDP technologies helps businesses **explore, develop, and prosper** through technology. We position as a trusted, senior, outcome-focused partner, not a commodity dev shop.

## Target audience (refine per beachhead)

- Startups and SMBs needing senior engineering, cloud/DevOps, AI, or security expertise.
- Companies undergoing digital transformation.

## Channels

| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Website (xdptechnologies.dev)** | Credibility, conversion, SEO home base |
| **Content / blog** | Demonstrate expertise; inbound SEO |
| **LinkedIn + founder-led content** | B2B reach, trust, network |
| **Case studies** | Social proof of outcomes |
| **Referrals / partnerships** | Highest-trust, lowest-CAC pipeline |
| **Community / open source** | Reputation among technical buyers |

## Content principles

- Teach, don't pitch. Show expertise through useful content.
- Turn every successful project into a case study (with client consent).
- Consistency beats intensity: a sustainable publishing cadence.

## Brand consistency

- All external content follows `company/brand-guidelines.md` (voice, naming, taglines).
- Always disambiguate from the Linux "XDP's" term by leading with "XDP technologies".

## Measuring marketing

- Track: website traffic, qualified leads, conversion rate, content engagement, CAC by channel.
- Double down on channels with the best CAC-to-LTV ratio.
