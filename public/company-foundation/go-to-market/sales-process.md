# Sales Process

## Philosophy

We sell by **solving problems**, not pushing services. Consultative, honest, and value-led. If we're not the right fit, we say so.

## Pipeline stages

| Stage | Definition | Exit criteria |
|---|---|---|
| **Lead** | Potential fit identified | Contact + qualified interest |
| **Discovery** | Understand problem, budget, timeline, authority | Qualified opportunity (BANT-style) |
| **Proposal** | Scope + pricing presented (SOW) | Proposal sent |
| **Negotiation** | Terms aligned | Verbal agreement |
| **Closed-Won/Lost** | Signed or declined | Contract signed / reason logged |

## Qualification

Qualify early to avoid wasting time. Confirm: real problem, budget, decision-maker, and timeline. Disqualify fast and politely when there's no fit.

## CRM & hygiene

- Track every opportunity in a CRM (or GitLab issues early on) with stage, value, and next step.
- Log activity; no opportunity without a defined next action and date.
- Review pipeline weekly.

## Proposals & contracts

- Proposals map to a clear SOW: scope, deliverables, timeline, price, assumptions, and exclusions.
- Use the standard MSA + SOW templates (`governance/legal-and-compliance.md`).
- Founders approve pricing/terms per the decision framework.

## Handoff to delivery

- On close, hand off cleanly to delivery with full context.
- Set expectations: kickoff, communication cadence, success criteria.
