# Equity & Cap Table Policy

> Internal policy. Confirm all specifics with legal and tax counsel.

## Principles

- The cap table is a **living, authoritative record** of who owns what. It is updated immediately after any equity event.
- All equity is subject to **vesting** to align incentives over time.
- Equity is precious. We grant it deliberately and document every grant.

## Founder equity

- Split defined in the Founders' Agreement based on contribution, role, and risk.
- **Standard vesting:** 4-year vest with a 1-year cliff (illustrative; confirm with counsel).
- Reverse-vesting / repurchase rights protect the company if a founder leaves early.

## Employee equity (ESOP)

- Establish an **Employee Stock Option Pool** (commonly 10–15% reserved) once hiring begins.
- Grants follow a documented band by level/role.
- Standard 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff.
- Maintain a written option grant policy and exercise window terms.

## Advisor & contractor equity

- Advisors: small grants (e.g., 0.1–1.0%) vesting over 1–2 years, tied to a written advisor agreement and expectations.
- Contractors are generally cash-only unless strategically critical.

## Cap table hygiene

- [ ] Single source of truth (cap table tool or maintained spreadsheet under founder control).
- [ ] Every grant backed by a signed agreement and board/founder approval.
- [ ] Fully-diluted view always available.
- [ ] Updated within 5 business days of any equity event.
- [ ] Reviewed before any fundraise.
