# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

XDP technologies is committed to a harassment-free, inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Treat everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Give credit; share knowledge.
- Assume good intent; communicate directly and kindly.
- Respect work-life boundaries and time zones.
- Protect confidential and customer information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind.
- Retaliation against anyone who raises a concern.
- Sharing confidential, customer, or proprietary data without authorization.
- Misrepresenting the company or its work.

## Reporting

- Report concerns to any Co-founder directly or via a designated confidential channel.
- All reports are taken seriously, handled confidentially where possible, and investigated promptly.
- No retaliation. Retaliation is itself a serious violation.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in corrective action up to and including termination of employment or contract, and where relevant, referral to authorities.
