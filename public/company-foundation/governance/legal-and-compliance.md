# Legal & Compliance

> This document is an internal operating guide, **not legal advice**. Engage qualified counsel before signing any binding document or incorporating.

## Incorporation checklist

- [ ] Choose legal structure and jurisdiction (consult counsel + tax advisor).
- [ ] Register the company and obtain a tax/registration ID.
- [ ] Open a business bank account (keep company and personal finances strictly separate).
- [ ] Execute the **Founders' Agreement** (equity split, vesting, roles, IP assignment, vesting cliff, leaver provisions, tie-breaker).
- [ ] Set up a **cap table** and keep it current.
- [ ] Assign all founder/employee/contractor IP to the company via signed agreements.
- [ ] Register trademark for "XDP technologies" in priority markets.
- [ ] Secure the `xdptechnologies.dev` domain and key social handles.

## Core legal documents to maintain

| Document | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Founders' Agreement | Equity, vesting, roles, dispute resolution |
| IP Assignment Agreements | Ensure company owns all work product |
| Employment Agreements | Terms for employees |
| Contractor/Consultant Agreements | Terms + IP assignment for contractors |
| NDA (mutual + one-way) | Protect confidential info |
| Master Services Agreement (MSA) | Standard terms for client work |
| Statement of Work (SOW) template | Scope, deliverables, fees per engagement |
| Terms of Service & Privacy Policy | Required for any product/website |
| Data Processing Agreement (DPA) | When handling customer personal data |

## Compliance posture

- **Data protection:** Follow applicable privacy law (e.g., GDPR-style principles) by default: lawful basis, data minimization, right to access/erasure. See `security/data-protection-and-privacy.md`.
- **Contracts:** No one signs on behalf of the company except authorized signatories (Co-founders) per the decision-making framework.
- **Records retention:** Keep signed agreements, financial records, and tax filings for the legally required period.
- **Conflicts of interest:** Disclose any conflict to the founders before acting.

## Insurance to evaluate

- Professional Indemnity / Errors & Omissions (critical for consulting/software work)
- General liability
- Cyber liability
- Directors & Officers (when board forms)
