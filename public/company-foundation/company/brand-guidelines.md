# Brand Guidelines

## Name usage

- Full legal/brand name: **XDP technologies**.
- Always write **"XDP technologies"** on first mention. "XDP's" alone may be used after the first mention within the same document.
- Never lowercase as "XDP's" in prose. Domains and handles may be lowercase.

### Disambiguation

"XDP's" is also the Linux networking term **eXpress Data Path**. To avoid confusion:
- Lead with "XDP technologies" in titles, ads, and SEO.
- Pair the name with our tagline where possible: *XDP technologies — Explore. Develop. Prosper.*

## Brand pillars

| Pillar | Meaning |
|---|---|
| eXplore | Curiosity, R&D, emerging tech |
| Develop | Engineering craft, delivery |
| Prosper | Customer and partner outcomes |

## Taglines

- Primary: **Explore. Develop. Prosper.**
- Alternates: *Engineering the Future.* / *Beyond Technology. Beyond Tomorrow.* / *Innovation Without Limits.* / *Building the Future, Together.*

## Voice & tone

- **Confident, not arrogant.** We know our craft; we stay humble about the customer's domain.
- **Clear over clever.** Plain language. Define jargon.
- **Optimistic and practical.** We sell outcomes, not hype.
